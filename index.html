HCV infection risk and decile calculator

This page scores a single patient locally from the 5-variable XGBoost model using birth year, sex, birthplace, race/ethnicity, and income-to-poverty ratio, then applies a calibrated risk mapping for bedside use. The modeled outcome is evidence of current or past HCV infection, not confirmed active viremia.

Use notes

  • This score estimates evidence of current or past HCV infection, meaning the patient may be currently infected or may have been infected previously.
  • A positive status screen still needs standard HCV testing workflow, typically antibody testing with confirmatory HCV RNA testing.
  • The calculator runs entirely in the browser after the page is opened.
  • Income should be entered as poverty-income ratio, not raw annual dollars.
